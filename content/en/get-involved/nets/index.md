<h1>Local Nets Around Iowa</h1>

{{% /blocks/lead %}}

{{< blocks/section color="white" >}}

<p>
  Across Iowa, amateur radio operators gather on local nets each week to share information,
  practice good operating habits, and strengthen our community. These nets are more than just
  check‑ins — they provide a vital opportunity to test equipment, stay familiar with emergency
  procedures, and connect with fellow hams across the state. Regular participation helps ensure
  that when communication really matters, we’re ready.
</p>
<p>
  If you’d like to have your net added to this list, please email
  <strong>community@k0iro.com</strong> with the details.
</p>
